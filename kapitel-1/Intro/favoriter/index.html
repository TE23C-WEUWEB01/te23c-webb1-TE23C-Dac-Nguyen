Among Us

Among Us is a survival horror game developed and published by The Crew, a subsidiary of Riot Games. The game is set in a large open-world, where players work together to survive in a hostile environment. Players must navigate through a series of challenges, such as teamwork, stealth, and avoiding unwanted attention.
